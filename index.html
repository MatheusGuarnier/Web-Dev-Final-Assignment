Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Matheus, a 22-year-old Brazilian on a thrilling journey at Bournemouth College. Dreaming big, my ultimate goal is to attend university, a dream that fuels my every step. Born in the vibrant state of Sao Paulo in 2001, I'm proud to carry the lively spirit of Brazil wherever I go. From the pulsating rhythms of Samba to the captivating dance moves and mouthwatering cuisine, Brazil's cultural treasures are my passion and inspiration.
