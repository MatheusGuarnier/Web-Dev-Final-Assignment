Sports

Since childhood, my parents instilled in me a love for sports, encouraging me to explore various disciplines. I've dabbled in volleyball, basketball, football, and swimming, taking lessons and even competing in local championships back in Brazil. Among them, volleyball resonated with me the most due to its competitive nature, igniting a passion for sports that drives me to excel.

Unfortunately, after relocating to England, I faced challenges in finding opportunities to pursue these sports and make new connections. However, I remain hopeful that one day I'll rekindle my athletic pursuits. Sports are not just a pastime for me, they're a lifelong passion that I eagerly anticipate rediscovering.
