Games

I've always been passionate about games. From my early years, my father instilled in me a love for video games. My very first gaming experience was with a ‘PlayStation 1’, where I cherished moments spent playing alongside my mum and dad. Fast forward to today, I've transitioned from console gaming to embracing computer games as my new obsession. Currently, my heart belongs to ‘League of Legends’, a MOBA offering matches lasting anywhere from 20 to 40 minutes, with no set maximum duration. Yet, ‘League of Legends’ isn't the sole focus of my gaming endeavors. I also delve into the worlds of Age of Empire 3 and The Finals with my friends. Each game offers a unique gameplay experience, but it's the diversity and endless possibilities that draw me in.
